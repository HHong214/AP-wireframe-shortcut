Booking instructions & policy

Grounded in campus://handbook
1. Hold = lock. Reserve holds a resource 10 min; persists if you close the window; one hold per person.
2. Members. Min count required; host added automatically; IDs must be valid, not banned/suspended, within daily limit.
3. Privacy. You see only the count of valid IDs and can only search people you've added before.
4. Duration. Min 30 min, max 2 hours; start in 15-min steps.
5. Gaps & void. You may book any free window; a leftover gap under 30 min is voided and can't be booked.
6. Confirmation. On confirm the system re-checks double-booking, verifies members, then emails everyone.
7. Hours. Booking available 24/7 within campus open hours; live help desk follows working hours.
